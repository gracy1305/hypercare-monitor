How I got here

The way I think about a company

Instead of sending the same application everywhere, I like to dig into how a company actually works, its customers, its stage, its likely bottlenecks, and then build a small proof of concept around what I find. It's not a portfolio trick. It's genuinely how I enjoy working.

1

Study the company

For EliseAI, that meant reading through the funding news, the housing and healthcare product lines, and especially EliseAI's own published research on AI adoption in affordable housing.

2

Find the real bottleneck

That research pointed to something specific. Trust in AI drops sharply around compliance sensitive topics, and EliseAI is onboarding new clients fast enough that manually watching every rollout doesn't scale.

3

Build something real

So I built a concept for exactly that. A tool an AI Ops Specialist could use to monitor a new client's first 60 days live, and catch the risky conversations before a resident or patient does.
